A commitment to diversity and equity. The network reflects an active commitment to diversity and equity in Brazilian science, composed of 62.5% women and 37.5% men researchers, with 31.3% of members self-declaring as Black. Its geographic distribution — spanning the Norte, Nordeste, Centro-Oeste, Sudeste and Sul regions — supports the goal of strengthening mathematical research capacity across all regions of Brazil.
